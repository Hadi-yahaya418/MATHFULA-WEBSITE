High School Geometry Equations

1. Lines and Angles

Slope of a line:
m = y2 - y1 x2 - x1
Equation of a line:
\[y = mx + b \] or \[y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \]
Distance between two points:
\[ d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \]
Midpoint of a line segment:
\[ \left(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right) \]
Sum of interior angles in a polygon:
\[(n - 2) \times 180^\circ \]
Sum of exterior angles in a polygon:
\[ 360^\circ \]

2. Triangles

Pythagorean Theorem:
\[ a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \]
Area of a triangle:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} \]
Area of a triangle (Heron's formula):
\[ A = \sqrt{s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)} \] where \[ s = \frac{a+b+c}{2} \]
Area of a triangle (using sine):
\[A = \frac{1}{2}ab\sin(C) \]
Sine rule:
\[\frac{a}{\sin(A)} = \frac{b}{\sin(B)} = \frac{c}{\sin(C)} \]
Cosine rule:
\[c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos(C) \]

3. Quadrilaterals

Area of a rectangle:
\[A = \text{length} \times \text{width} \]
Area of a square:
\[A = \text{side}^2 \]
Area of a parallelogram:
\[A = \text{base} \times \text{height} \]
Area of a trapezoid:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} \times (\text{base}_1 + \text{base}_2) \times \text{height} \]
Area of a rhombus:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{diagonal}_1 \times \text{diagonal}_2 \]

4. Circles

Circumference of a circle:
\[C = 2\pi r \]
Area of a circle:
\[A = \pi r^2 \]
Arc length:
\[L = \theta r \] (where \( \theta \) is in radians)
Area of a sector:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} r^2 \theta \] (where \( \theta \) is in radians)
Area of a segment:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} r^2 (\theta - \sin(\theta)) \]

5. Polygons

Area of a regular polygon:
\[A = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{perimeter} \times \text{apothem} \]
Number of diagonals in a polygon:
\[\frac{n(n-3)}{2} \] (where \( n \) is the number of sides)

6. Solid Geometry

Surface area of a cube:
\[A = 6s^2 \]
Volume of a cube:
\[V = s^3 \]
Surface area of a rectangular prism:
\[A = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh \]
Volume of a rectangular prism:
\[V = l \times w \times h \]
Surface area of a cylinder:
\[A = 2\pi r^2 + 2\pi rh \]
Volume of a cylinder:
\[V = \pi r^2 h \]
Surface area of a cone:
\[A = \pi r (r + l) \] (where \( l \) is the slant height)
Volume of a cone:
\[V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \]
Surface area of a sphere:
\[A = 4\pi r^2 \]
Volume of a sphere:
\[V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \]
Surface area of a pyramid:
\[A = B + \frac{1}{2} \times \text{perimeter} \times \text{slant height} \]
Volume of a pyramid:
\[V = \frac{1}{3} \times \text{Base Area} \times \text{height} \]

7. Coordinate Geometry

Equation of a circle:
\[(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2 \] (where \( (h, k) \) is the center and \( r \) is the radius)
Equation of a parabola:
\[y = ax^2 + bx + c \] or \[(x-h)^2 = 4p(y-k) \]
Equation of an ellipse:
\[\frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2} + \frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2} = 1 \]
Equation of a hyperbola:
\[\frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2} - \frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2} = 1 \]
